About Us

Slice O Heaven Pizza is a family-owned business that has been serving delicious pizza for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide high-quality, fresh ingredients and excellent service to our customers.

Our pizzas are made with only the finest ingredients, including locally-sourced vegetables and meats. We also offer a variety of other menu items, including salads, sandwiches, and desserts.

At Slice O Heaven, we pride ourselves on our commitment to the community. We participate in local events and fundraisers, and we also donate a portion of our profits to local charities.

Thank you for choosing Slice O Heaven Pizza. We appreciate your business and look forward to serving you!
